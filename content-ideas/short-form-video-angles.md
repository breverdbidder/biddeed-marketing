## Video Concepts

### Pillar 1: Deal Outcomes

#### Video: "This AI Found $100K I Would Have Lost"
**Duration:** 30-45 seconds  
**Hook:** "This property looked perfect. $180K judgment, $320K ARV, easy flip..."  
**Reveal:** "Then my AI found this." [Show HOA as plaintiff]  
**Education:** "HOA foreclosure. Senior mortgage survives. I'd inherit $200K in debt."  
**Punch:** "BECA Scraper caught it in 8 seconds. SKIP."  
**CTA:** "Comment 'LIEN' to see how it works."

#### Video: "I Bought This House for 40% of Value"
**Duration:** 30 seconds  
**Hook:** Show property exterior  
**Flow:** Quick walkthrough of BID recommendation → Auction day → Winning bid  
**Result:** Purchase price vs ARV comparison  
**CTA:** "Follow for more foreclosure wins"

### Pillar 2: Risk Avoidance

#### Video: "3 Properties That Would Have Bankrupted Me"
**Duration:** 60 seconds  
**Format:** Quick cuts between three properties  
**Property 1:** Tax certificate with $40K in prior liens  
**Property 2:** IRS federal tax lien surviving foreclosure  
**Property 3:** Code enforcement liens totaling $85K  
**Punch:** "AI caught all three. $225K in losses avoided this month."

#### Video: "Why 'Good Deals' Kill Investors"
**Duration:** 45 seconds  
**Hook:** "This deal had 65% equity. Everyone bid on it."  
**Reveal:** "Everyone except me." [Show SKIP recommendation]  
**Education:** Explain hidden lien or title issue  
**Result:** "Winning bidder just discovered they owe $X more"

## Platform-Specific Adaptations

### YouTube Shorts
- 60 seconds max
- Vertical format (9:16)
- Hook in first 2 seconds
- End with subscribe reminder

### TikTok
- 30-45 seconds optimal
- Trending audio overlay
- Text on screen for sound-off viewers
- Duet/stitch potential

### LinkedIn
- 45-90 seconds
- Professional tone
- Results-focused
- Comment engagement questions

### X/Twitter
- Under 2:20 for native video
- Real-time auction commentary
- Thread potential for deeper dives
